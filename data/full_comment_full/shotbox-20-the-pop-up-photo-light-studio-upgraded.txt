When items are shipped to the EU we have to pay duty and tax (rates differ between countries). To make these calculations it is important customs know what the product value is (invoice) and also what the shippingcost are. If shippingcost are not put on the form, the importing shipping company (DHL for example) will calculate the tax based on their regular fee. For example if I pay $30 for shipping, but the documentation does not state the shippingcost I paid. DHL will charge tax over $189, which is their regular rate for a 14 pound package from the US to The Netherlands. So please don't forget to also declare what we paid for shipping.
